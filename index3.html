    Test Conducted

    Here is the series of successfull test conducted by us

    VSLV 1.0

    VSLV 1.0 successfully test fired on 22 August 8:15 AM IST ,upto height of 800m. Mission 1 tested Launch capability with Basic electronics (GPS, Altimeter & parachute ejection system).

    VSLV 2.0

    VSLV 2.0 successfully test fired on 1 January 11:50 AM IST,upto height of 1.7km. In Mission 2 Higher altitude was achieved with fully working payload. Payload Features: Altitude measurement, Pressure measurement, Air speed measurement, Temperature and Humidity measuring in ambient atmosphere.

    VSLV 3.0

    VSLV 3.0 GRAVITION succesfully fierd on 30 March 2018 at 08:31:53 IST,upto height of 3.89km. In Mission 3 altitude of 13000ft (4km) was achieved with Upgraded Rocket Structure and advanced payload. A Specially designed algorithm to work with GSM Module, which gathered data on cellular signal strength and network.

    VSLV 3.1

    VSLV 3.1 successfully fired on 10 September 2018 at 11:03 IST,upto height of 3.91km. The same apogee was achieved with Modifications in Propellant for greater thrust. On board Flight Camera and In-house Ground station was setup.

    VSLV 3.2

    VSLV 3.2 successfully test fired on 25th November 2018 at 11:35 AM IST,upto height of 1km. A series of 3.X Launches to test various subsystems, then integrate it for our next (fourth) mission. Ejection and Dual Deployment of Parachute was tested along with Live Telemetry and Video feed.
