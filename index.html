About VaSSaUnT:-

VASSAUNT is the annual social and cultural festival of Veer Surendra Sai University of Technology,Burla. A dazzling and glamorous cultural festival in western odisha and has set a trademark ever since its initiation. This year VASSAUNT comes with Bollywood ishstyle theme which will provide a complete package of joy, esctacy and euphoria. So, be ready to lose yourself in 3 days of fun.
