    >Weave-a-gic

    There is more to life than just being realistic & making sense. So if you wield the portal to a secret universe with your spells of ink, we shall consider you worthy. Come with us, give wings to your words and weave magic. The creative writing competition awaits you to unfurl your spells.

    Open mic

    Picturesque

    Beauty lies in the eyes of the beholder. Nothing trumps this when it comes to the art of reading between the lines. We'll put you against a series of imagery for you to narrate us a story. A picture is surely worth a zillion of words but can you draw an ace among everyone else? Let's find out.

    Impromptu

    Time is a luxury here. We often reveal ourselves when push comes to shove and what better occasion than this. Turn on the orator mode and show them who is the king of the arena. Let them know, you are not the one to be messed with because you are always ready.

    Architect of Lexicon

    There's more than one reason we know the word "tharoor" for. Owning the dictionary isn't everyone's game but those who own it know how to do the KILL. It's your chance to be coronated as the Emperor of Lexicon. Your vocabulary prowess will be tested against tricky rules, and we're ready to see how you fare.

    What the Word

    What's the big deal with a word? Ah! Sure, not much but what if we put you under the dumb spell. Dumb Charades. Crosswords and other games that test you to put on your creative skills with words. In the tint of Bollywood, we bring to you this amazingly fun event. We can't wait to turn on the fun gear.

    Spell Bee

    This one is a total no brainer. No tricks. No gimmicks. Yet, the stuff gets out of hand pretty often pretty fast. A spelling nazi by heart and an avid reader by practice can only come back victorious from this Den of Spell. You'll need the dictionary here again except for every letter will matter.
